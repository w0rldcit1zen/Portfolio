04

Social Listening & Reputational Management

JD: "Monitor online conversations and sentiment. Conduct reputational risk assessments. Coordinate responses to reputational risks."

Social listening is about following the conversations that matter — especially around key moments like report launches and flagship publications. We don't want the conversation we're advancing to be hijacked, and with misinformation and disinformation growing across all channels, monitoring is essential. It was actually through moderating social media and tracking a growing trend of climate disinformation that I was able to brief and convince senior leadership at WMO to join the UN's Global Initiative on Information Integrity. Social listening didn't just flag a risk — it led to an institutional decision.

That same awareness of how misinformation spreads is what led me to build an AI-powered chatbot trained on WMO's policy documents — making authoritative information more accessible at a time when it was needed most. The chatbot earned an award from the Royal Meteorological Society. Social listening informs everything: from daily community management to innovation pivots that update the entire digital strategy.

06

Analytics, Reporting & Optimisation

JD: "Use digital analytics tools to generate performance dashboards. Track KPIs and adjust tactics."

A/B testing is at the heart of everything. It determines what we start, stop, or continue. You take out the emotion, you look at the data, you see what works. You stay open to experimentation, to new things. And when something works, you scale it. That's it.

For reporting, I keep it monthly — regular check-ins to see how we're tracking, spot spikes, and course-correct. But I also report around key moments: flagship publications, major events, campaign milestones — whenever the data tells a story worth sharing. And then one comprehensive year-end report that pulls it all together, including lessons learned that shape the next year's strategy.

Methodology

Audit → Test → Optimise cycle

Baseline audit → A/B testing → monthly check-in reports → start/stop/continue content reviews → annual social media report with lessons learned. Repeatable cycle designed to compound results year over year.
